# Nature pack (CC0)

Quaternius-style stylized nature: birch trees, flowering bushes, flower clumps.
.gltf + .bin + shared textures (BirchTree_*, Bush_Leaves, Flowers). Scattered as
bank/meadow accents on top of the instanced procedural forest (world/vegetation.js).

License: CC0 (public domain). Drop-in via src/assets/manifest.js (nat_* ids).
