  (also https://kaylousberg.itch.io/spacebase-bits)

Modern future-tech kit used for the research facilities + props. Each `.gltf` references
the shared `spacebits_texture.png` + its `.bin` by relative path, so they must stay
co-located. Wired via `src/assets/manifest.js` (glass_tower/ring_lab → basemodule_A/C,
plus space_solar/space_pad/space_rover). Same art family as the City Builder set for
visual cohesion.
