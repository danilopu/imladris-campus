# Village buildings (CC0)

KayKit-style low-poly medieval/builder modules (FirstAge set): houses, tower-house,
market, dock, farm, windmill, barrel/crate/logs. Self-contained .gltf (geometry +
flat baseColor materials embedded). Used for the riverside hamlet, market, dock,
footbridge gate-tower, and the Wildwood chicken farm.
